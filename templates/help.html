Help & FAQs

Here are some common questions about using Biosafety EL.

How do I check a product?

Enter the product’s barcode in the search field and click “Analyze”.

What data sources are used?

We use OpenFoodFacts for product information and OpenFDA for compliance data.

Why is my product not found?

If a barcode is missing from OpenFoodFacts, you can contribute it directly on their site!
